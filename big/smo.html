Social Media Optimization Research

Social Media Optimization, or SMO for short, is a strategy to increase awareness of your company, brand, or event on the internet and its applications. To quote Dominique Jackson, “Social Media Optimization is essentially using social media as a catalyst to grow your company’s online presence. Where some companies tend to just set up a profile on Instagram, Facebook or Twitter to be where their customers are, SMO is about strategically creating, building and maximizing your social media plan to connect with your target audience.” Considering that there are so many different social media platforms that are currently being used, a good social media strategy will employ tactics to interact with all, or many, of these platforms. To expand a website’s public presence, a strong social media optimization strategy must be employed. A good SMO is “the most effective way to strengthen a brand, conduct lead generation, increase a company's visibility in the online space, and connect to an audience. Various social media platforms can be used for digital marketing, including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube, and Pinterest” (Kenton). Some simple social media optimization tactics include knowing and defining your key audience, and employing social media to appeal to them directly. You can also be very clear about knowing when to post, since some hours of the day have much higher views. There are also many artificial intelligence tools and applications to enable daily, scheduled posts. However, one of the most important SMO tactics is to constantly update and revise your social media presence. Since social media is so quickly evolving, it is important to stay current and connected with the public.
