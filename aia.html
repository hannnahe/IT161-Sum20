AIA- Audience, Issues and Approach

Client Information

Client's Name: Katherine Fox
Website Name: Katherine Fox Photography
Redesign or Brand New Website: Brand New Website
Type of Website: Creative Portfolio
Client's Goals: The client needs a platform to showcase her work, both current and future. She will use it to document her work through a training program at Seattle Central. This will be a website that is easy to update and continue to add to. She will also use it to make contacts, broadcast her work, and have a marketing presence on the internet.

Audience, Issues, Approach

Audience Issues Approach
Instructors:
Since this website will directly be used as a working portfolio, the instructors and professors in her college will use this website to track her progress and projects. These instructors are photographers and graphic designers, so will have very high technical knowledge. They will likely access this website on desktop computers, both PC and Mac, but could also access using other portals such as an iPad or phone, with varying monitor resolution sizes. Most instructors will be working from a desk, in which case they may have larger monitors to view the website. The instructors will use a variety of browsers, including but not limited to: IE, Firefox, Chrome, Safari. 		Instructors are using this website to track and perhaps grade the student’s work. In this portfolio, they will want to see:
  • Examples of her photography and editing skills
  • Different navigation menus containing past and current projects
  • Concise Resume and/or CV
  • Easy and clear navigation through the site
  • Excellent quality resolution on all photography
  • Ability to download Katherine’s photos with her permission
  • Concise resume/ CV page
  • Navigation menu in top or side of page
  • Multiple pages of past and current projects, all in great resolution
  • Downloads (with authors permission) page of her work
Past Clients:
The website will be used to document photography projects for past clients, such as commercial photography clients. These companies may use the website to re-visit these commercial shoots and the photographs produced. They should have medium to advanced computer skills, and will access the site on many browsers over PCs, Macs, iPhones, and Ipads, among other portals of varying resolution sizes. 		Past clients will use the site to re-connect with Katherine, or revisit the commercial photography she shot for their company. They will need:
  • Contact information for Katherine
  • Concise Resume and/or CV
  • Easy and clear navigation through the site
  • Great documentation on all past and present projects
  • Excellent quality resolution on all photography
  • Ability to download Katherine’s photos with her permission
  • Contact page for Katherine
  • Concise resume/ CV page
  • Navigation menu in top or side of page
  • Multiple pages of past and current projects, all in great resolution
  • Downloads (with authors permission) page of her work
Prospective Employers and Clients:
This group's profile is quite similar to that of the past clients, except they will be using this portfolio to decide whether to hire the photographer or not. They will have high attention to detail, and likely quite strong technical skills. They will use tablets, phones, and computers to access the site, including android and macs, across varying resolutions. 		Prospective Employers and clients will use this site to learn more about Katherine, her work, and her experience, and perhaps use it to contact her regarding jobs or employment. They will need:
  • An “About the Photographer” page to learn about Katherines experience in more detail
  • Contact information for Katherine
  • Concise Resume and/or CV
  • Easy and clear navigation through the site
  • Great documentation on all past and present projects
  • Excellent quality resolution on all photography
  • Ability to download Katherine’s photos with her permission
  • An About The Photographer page
  • Contact page for Katherine
  • Concise resume/ CV page
  • Navigation menu in top or side of page
  • Multiple pages of past and current projects, all in great resolution
  • Downloads (with authors permission) page of her work
